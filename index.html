Scrambled Eggs

Eggs are one of my favorite foods. Here is the recipe for deliciously rich scrambled eggs.

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1tbs butter
  • 2tbs cream

Method

  1. Melt butter in a frying pan over a medium heat.
  2. Gently mix the eggs and cream in a bowl.
  3. Once butter has melted add cream and eggs.
  4. Using spatula fold the eggs from the edge of the pan to the center every 20 seconds(as if you making omelette).
  5. When the eggs are still moist remove the heat(it will continue to cook on the plate and served).
